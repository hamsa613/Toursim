Tourism Types

Beach Tourism

Enjoy the sun, sand and sea with beach tourism. Relax on the beach or take part in water sports like surfing, snorkeling and scuba diving.

Adventure Tourism

For thrill-seekers, adventure tourism offers activities like bungee jumping, rock climbing, white-water rafting and zip-lining.

Cultural Tourism

Explore the history and traditions of a place with cultural tourism. Visit museums, art galleries and historical sites to learn about the local culture.

Ecotourism

Experience nature and wildlife with ecotourism. Go on a safari, hike through a national park or visit an animal sanctuary to see animals in their natural habitat.
